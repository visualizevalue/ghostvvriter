# The Time Ladder

A framework for understanding how the value of your time changes as you build leverage.

## The Rungs

1. **Your time is worthless** — You're unknown. No proof, no reputation. Do free work to build both.
2. **Your time is cheap** — You have some proof. Clients pay, but below market rate. Keep building.
3. **Your time is expensive** — You have a reputation. Demand exceeds supply. You can be selective.
4. **Your time is not for sale** — You've built systems (products, content, equity) that generate value without your direct involvement. Time is fully yours.

## The Mechanism

Each rung is unlocked by accumulating proof at the current one. Free work creates proof. Proof creates demand. Demand creates pricing power. Pricing power funds products. Products buy back your time.

## Key Insight

Most people try to price their time as expensive before they've earned the proof. The ladder only works in order. You can't skip rungs — but you can climb them faster by working in public.
