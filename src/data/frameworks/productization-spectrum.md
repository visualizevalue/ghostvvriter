# The Productization Spectrum

The system behind Visualize Value's path from one-person studio to 7-figure product business.

## The Spectrum

1. **Service** — Work without process. Getting specific knowledge. You trade time for money with no leverage.
2. **Productized Service** — Work with process. Stabilizing time & income. You've systematized delivery.
3. **Product** — Process without work. Buying back your time. The system runs without you.

## Key Principles

### The Proof & Price Loop
Give it away until you have enough proof to sell it. Get proof, get paid, get more proof, get paid more.

### Selling Sawdust
Share the byproduct of your work. Everything you do is valuable to two audiences: clients who trust you can do it for them, and customers who trust you can teach them.

### Build Once, Sell Twice
Create the product, productize the process. Sell both. Your work product and the system that creates it are two separate revenue streams.

### Ownership x Time = Freedom
The more you own of what produces value, and the longer you compound it, the freer you become.
