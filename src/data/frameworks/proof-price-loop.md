# The Proof & Price Loop

A flywheel for pricing your work based on demonstrated results rather than claimed expertise.

## The Loop

1. **Give it away** — Do free work to generate proof (results, testimonials, case studies).
2. **Get proof** — Document the outcome. The proof is the product of the work.
3. **Get paid** — Use the proof to justify a price. Start low.
4. **Get more proof** — Paid work generates better proof (real clients, real results).
5. **Get paid more** — Better proof justifies higher prices.
6. **Repeat** — The loop compounds. Each cycle increases both proof and price.

## Key Insight

Most people try to set prices based on what they think they're worth. The market doesn't care what you think. It cares what you can prove. Let the proof set the price.

## Application

This loop works at every stage of the Time Ladder:
- **Worthless → Cheap**: Free work builds initial proof
- **Cheap → Expensive**: Paid results build premium proof
- **Expensive → Not for sale**: Systematized proof becomes a product
